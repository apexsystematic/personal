Sun Safety
for Kai

18 months · Beach Guide for Mom & Dad

🟢 Low UV 1–2
  • No special precautions needed
  • Sunblock optional but fine to use
  • Enjoy freely!
🟡 Moderate UV 3–5
  • SPF 30+ sunblock — reapply every 2 hrs or after water
  • Cover ears, back of neck, tops of feet
  • Hat if you can get it on him 😄
  • Offer water frequently — he won't ask
  • Morning timing is ideal
🟠 High / Very High UV 6–10
  • Stick to before 10am or after 4pm
  • Shade first (umbrella, tent) — sunblock is backup, not primary
  • SPF 30+ still required
  • Lightweight cover-up clothing where possible
  • Limit duration, watch for overheating closely
🟣 Extreme UV 11+
  • Keep Kai indoors during peak daylight hours
  • If unavoidable: full shade only, sunblock, cover-up, brief exposure
  • Not worth the risk at his age
Always — regardless of UV
  • Reapply sunblock every 2 hrs and after water
  • Offer water constantly — toddlers don't signal thirst reliably
  • Morning beach is your best default ✓
Prepared by Dr. Mia · Based on AAP & WHO guidelines
